New England Cottontail: Pregnancy Stress Relief

Project done in partnership (Tinsley Huo) with assistance and feedback from: Harpreet Sareen, Gabriel Miller, Scott Silver, and Lou Perrotti.


Final Presentation PDF: Expand

This final prototype for the New England Cottontail captive breeding program utilizes commercially available rabbit calming music paired with a delivery device specifically designed for the NEC's captive breeding environment. With proven research showing lower cortisol levels in rabbits who were exposed to the music, the hope is that pregnant mothers will have lower stress levels and therefore be less likely to exhibit harmful or neglectful behavior towards their young in the first pregancy. Special thanks to direction and feedback from Lou Perrotti of the Roger Williams Zoo and Scott Silver of the Queens Zoo.
